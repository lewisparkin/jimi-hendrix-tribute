The life of Jimi Hendrix

1942 - Born in Seattle

1957 - Hendrix found a Ukelele with only one string and learned songs on it by ear.

1958 - Hendrix, at age 15, bought his first guitar, an acoustic, for $5 (equivalent to ~$50 today).

~1958 - Formed his first band "Velvetones".

1959 - Hendrix's Dad bought Jimi his first electric guitar.

1961 - Hendrix enlists in the Army to avoid jail time for riding in stolen cars.

1962 - Hendrix is honourably discharged from the Army because of his lack of interest.

1964 - Hendrix recorded a two-part single with the Isley Brothers.

1966 - Hendrix moves to London and starts The Jimi Hendrix Experience.

1967 - "Are You Experienced" is released in the UK

1967 - "Axis: Bold as Love" is released spending 16 weeks on the charts.

1968 - Electric Ladyland is released.

1969 - Hendrix became the world's highest paid rock musician.

1970 - Hendrix is pronounced dead at the age of 27.
